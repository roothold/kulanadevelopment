Who We Are

A new development company, built around a clear vision.

Kulana Development brings entitlements, design coordination, procurement, and site execution under a single, accountable team. Our vision is simple and uncompromising: to develop amenity-rich communities designed to perform for decades — not just to open.

Our Discipline

Development, done with precision.

Every development begins with rigorous feasibility and a clear-eyed view of the market. We align capital, design, and construction from day one — so decisions compound in your favor instead of surfacing as surprises later.

The result is a development process that protects value at every stage: entitlements secured, budgets held, timelines met, and quality that speaks for itself.

The Core of Our Value

Amenity and thriving community.

Amenities and thriving communities are the heart of everything we build. We don't just deliver residences — we create the daily experience of living there: resort-caliber pools, wellness and fitness, chef-grade social kitchens, coworking, and programming that turns neighbors into a community.

This is where enduring value is created. A thriving community drives absorption, commands premium rents, and keeps residents rooted for years.
